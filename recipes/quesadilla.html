Quesadilla

Description

Quesadillas are the best, you have the protein, the carbs, the ingredients and the time

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • ham
  • cheese

Steps

  1. cut the cheese
  2. put the tortilla in the fire
  3. put the ham and cheese on top of the tortilla and fold it in two
  4. move it so the tortilla doesn't get burned and wait untill the cheese melts
