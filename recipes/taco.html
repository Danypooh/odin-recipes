Taco

Description

Tacos are so simple to make and to put it simply: the best f****** food in the entire multiverse.

Ingredients

  • "pastor" meet
  • onion
  • perejil
  • pineapple
  • salsa
    • tomatoe
    • chile
  • tortilla

Steps

  1. cut the meet
  2. cut the onion in little pieces
  3. cut the perejil in little pieces
  4. cut the pineapple in little pieces
  5. make the salsa
  6. put the ingredients in the following order:
    1. tortilla
    2. meet
    3. salsa
    4. perejil, onions and pineapple
